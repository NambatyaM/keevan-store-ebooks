### Overview

Keevan Store is a production ready digital commerce platform purpose built for East African creators to sell ebooks, guides, templates, and other digital products directly to their audience. The platform handles the complete transaction lifecycle: storefront creation, secure file uploads, payment processing via Pesapal in UGX and KES, automated digital delivery, email confirmations, and creator payout management.

Built with Next.js 15, Supabase, and TypeScript and live in production at keevanstore.in, Keevan Store presents a unique acquisition opportunity for companies looking to enter East Africa's growing creator economy. The platform requires zero additional development for launch: it is fully functional with payment integration, email delivery, an admin dashboard, and analytics.

The platform is sold as a complete business asset including full source code ownership, brand and domain assets, production infrastructure, database architecture, payment integrations, documentation, and knowledge transfer. The asset includes 30 database migrations, 609 automated tests, and a live production deployment.
