### Business Model

Keevan Store operates on a commission based revenue model typical of creator commerce platforms. The platform takes a 10% fee on every transaction processed through the platform. This model aligns platform incentives with creator success: as creators sell more, platform revenue grows.

The platform currently supports transactions in UGX and KES, with additional currencies (TZS, RWF, USD) available for future expansion.

### Cost Structure

#### Current Platform Costs

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Vercel Hosting | $20 per month (Hobby plan) |
| Supabase Database | Free tier (500 MB database, 2 GB storage) |
| Resend Email | 100 free emails per day |
| Pesapal Payments | Transaction fees passed to buyer |
| Domain (keevanstore.in) | Standard domain renewal |
| Sentry Monitoring | Free tier for error tracking |

The platform has near zero marginal cost per transaction. All variable costs are limited to Vercel compute time for serverless function execution and Resend email volume. The platform can scale to hundreds of transactions before incurring significant infrastructure costs.
