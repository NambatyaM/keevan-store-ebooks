### Phase 1: Launch (COMPLETE)

The foundational platform has been built, tested, and deployed to production. All core commerce functionality is operational:

- Creator registration, storefront creation, and product upload and publishing
- Buyer storefront browsing, checkout, and Pesapal payment processing in UGX and KES
- Automated digital delivery with email confirmations and secure download links
- Admin dashboard for platform management with user, order, and store oversight
- Creator dashboard with analytics, earnings tracking, and discount management
- Buyer dashboard with purchase history and download access
- Security: RLS, CSRF protection, rate limiting, file validation, and RBAC
- Testing: 609 automated tests across 27 suites ensuring platform reliability
- Deployment: Vercel production deployment with automatic CI/CD pipeline
- Multi currency support: UGX, KES, TZS, RWF, USD
- 30 database migrations documenting complete schema evolution
- Payment integration with Pesapal IPN webhooks and transaction verification
