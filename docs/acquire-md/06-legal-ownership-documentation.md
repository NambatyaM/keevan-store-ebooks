### Exclusions

The following are not included in the acquisition:

- Ongoing development services beyond the two week knowledge transfer period
- Future feature development, customization, or enhancement work beyond the existing codebase
- Third party service subscriptions beyond their existing billing terms (acquirer will need to assume or establish their own accounts)
- Existing creator relationships or contracts (creators use the platform as a service and will need to be onboarded to the acquirer's terms)
- Ongoing hosting costs (acquirer assumes Vercel, Supabase, Resend, Sentry, and domain costs post transfer)
