>
> Feel free to check it out anytime at keevanstore.in.

### If They Say "How is this different from [competitor]?"
> Great question. Here's how Keevan Store compares:
>
> - **vs WhatsApp selling** — automated delivery, no manual chasing, payment verification before download
> - **vs international platforms** — mobile money support (MTN, Airtel), UGX pricing, no USD conversion
> - **vs marketplace** — you own your store, you control your audience, no algorithm deciding who sees your work
> - **vs direct bank transfers** — instant automated delivery, professional storefront, secure payment processing
