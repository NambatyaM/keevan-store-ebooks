## 4. Outreach Sequence by Channel

### Email Outreach Sequence

**Day 1 — Initial Email**
- Reference specific company initiative or strategy
- State value proposition clearly
- Call to action: 15-minute call or reply with interest

**Day 7 — Follow-up #1**
- Reference previous email
- Add one new piece of information or social proof
- Call to action: request for brief call

**Day 14 — Follow-up #2 (Final)**
- Brief, respectful
- Offer to close or ask for referral
- Call to action: reply or introduction

**After response:**
- Schedule call within 48 hours
- Prepare personalized pitch using Part 5 materials
- Send data room under NDA before or during call
- Follow up within 24 hours of call with summary and next steps

### LinkedIn Outreach Sequence

**Day 1 — Connection Request**
- Include personalized note: "Hi [Name], I've been following [Company]'s work in [space]. I built a creator commerce platform for East Africa and would love to connect. — [Name]"

**Day 3 — After Connection Accepted**
- Send InMail using Part 4 template
- Reference their role and company
- Clear call to action

**Day 10 — Follow-up Message**
- Brief reminder
- New information or angle
- Offer to share documentation

**Day 21 — Final Message**
- Respectful close
- Ask for referral if not a fit

### Twitter/X Outreach Sequence

**Day 1 — Initial DM**
- Short (280 characters preferred)
- Direct value proposition
- Ask if they're open to a conversation

**Day 5 — Follow-up**
- Share a link to the platform or a relevant post
- Restate interest briefly

**Day 14 — Final follow-up**
- Brief goodbye message
- Leave door open for future contact

## 5. Call Preparation Checklist

### Before the Call

- [ ] Research company thoroughly (complete research sheet above)
- [ ] Research the specific person (LinkedIn, recent posts, common connections)
- [ ] Review the appropriate outreach template and personalize for this conversation
- [ ] Prepare talking points from Part 5
- [ ] Have platform ready to show (if appropriate)
- [ ] Prepare answers to likely questions (see Part 5 FAQ)
- [ ] Have data room access ready
- [ ] Test audio/video if virtual call
- [ ] Prepare specific ask (next step you want after this call)

### Call Structure (20-minute intro call)

**0-2 min: Opening**
- Thank them for their time
- Brief personal introduction
- Set the stage: "I'd like to share what I built and why I think it might be relevant to [Company]"

**2-10 min: Overview**
- Problem -> Solution -> Traction -> Strategic Value
- Focus on what's most relevant to this specific company
- Use talking points from Part 5

**10-15 min: Q&A**
- Answer questions honestly
- If you don't know something, say so and offer to follow up
- Listen for signals of interest

**15-20 min: Next Steps**
- If interested: propose next meeting with appropriate team members
- If undecided: offer to send data room and follow up
- If not interested: ask for referrals
- Confirm next steps in writing after call

### After the Call

- [ ] Send thank-you email within 2 hours
- [ ] Include summary of key points discussed
- [ ] Confirm next steps with timeline
- [ ] Send data room link if agreed
- [ ] Update tracking log
- [ ] Add notes to CRM or tracking document
