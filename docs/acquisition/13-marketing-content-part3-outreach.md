# Acquisition Marketing Content — Part 3: Cold Outreach Messages

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** July 2026  
**Target:** Keevan Store — East African Creator Commerce Platform

---

## Style Guide

All messages follow these principles:
- Respectful, professional, concise
- Lead with strategic value, not revenue
- Transparent about stage (early but validated)
- Specific about what you're offering and what you need
- Include clear call to action
- Personalize before sending (anchor text in brackets)
