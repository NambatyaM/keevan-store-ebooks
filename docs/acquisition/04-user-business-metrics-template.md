## 6. Recommended Additional KPIs

The following KPIs are not currently tracked but are recommended for comprehensive business intelligence:

### 6.1 Growth KPIs
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** — Measure creator and buyer satisfaction
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** — Cost to acquire a new creator or buyer
- **Creator Lifetime Value (LTV)** — Total platform fees generated per creator
- **Buyer Lifetime Value** — Total purchase value per buyer
- **Viral coefficient** — Number of new users referred per existing user

### 6.2 Platform Health KPIs
- **Platform uptime** — Track via external monitoring service (e.g., Better Uptime, Pingdom)
- **API response times** — Track p50/p95/p99 latency
- **Error rate** — Percentage of failed API requests
- **Email delivery rate** — Percentage of queued emails successfully sent
- **Payment success rate** — Percentage of payment attempts that complete successfully

### 6.3 Creator-Specific KPIs
- **Time to first sale** — Average time from creator signup to first purchase
- **Activation rate** — % of creators who upload at least one product
- **Product publish rate** — % of uploaded products that get published
- **Store completion rate** — % of creators who complete their store profile

### 6.4 Buyer-Specific KPIs
- **Checkout abandonment rate** — % of users who start checkout but don't complete payment
- **Download rate** — % of paid orders where the file is downloaded
- **Refund rate** — % of paid orders that are refunded
- **Review rate** — % of purchasers who leave a review

### 6.5 Product-Specific KPIs
- **Price elasticity** — How price changes affect conversion rates
- **Category distribution** — Which product types sell best
- **Seasonal trends** — Monthly/seasonal sales patterns
